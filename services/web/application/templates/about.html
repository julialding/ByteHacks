Our Mission

The Great Depression was a drastic economic catastrophe that took place in the 1930s as a result of a huge stock market crash, causing several dreadful consequences that resulted in sky-high unemployment rates globally. Banks failed, investors were wiped out, and over 15 million Americans were left jobless. Those who were able to keep their job earned a much lower salary, and many descended into debt, resulting in states of absolute poverty.

But what if the technology present today was available in that period of history? The search for jobs would have been so much easier nationwide. Helploy is a website designed to help the economic situation by alleviating the severity of unemployment. Helploy allows the input of oneâ€™s personal information through their own personal account, which contains important information such as location, job desired, previous experience, etc. Helploy also takes into consideration various discriminatory factors that were prevalent back then, such as gender and race. Based on all this information, Helploy matches the job-seeker to a job, thereby increasing the chances of employment.

The ongoing global pandemic has caused increasing rates of unemployment, resulting in the financial instability of many households. Not surprisingly, the applications of Helploy are still relevant today.

Helploy was created as a product of ByteHacks 2020 , an online hackathon.
